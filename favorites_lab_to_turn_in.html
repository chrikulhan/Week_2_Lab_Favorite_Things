Favorite Movie

One of my favorite documentaries is titled Iris about a (then) 93 year old icon named Iris Apfel. She started a textile company with her husband and worked with 9 presidential administrations updating various White House furniture. Her bold use of color, accessories and playful sizes, and her quirky personality made her a fashion model in her 90s. Her 101st birthday was on August 29th, 2022.
